About me

A small introduction about myself

Reece B.King

An Engineering Technologist from Redditch in the UK

I am a technology enthusiast at heart and love anything related to it. In my spare time I enjoy Website development, app development, image manipulation, electronics, hardware repair and weightlifting. I am keen to learn new things which is why I have a large range of skills relating to technology. I am competent at soldering and am able to repair a range of hardware ranging from small devices (phone, tablets) all the way to large devices (TVs). I also modify existing devices as a hobby and enjoy all aspects of hardware as well as software and I am also able to read PCB schematics.

I'm a highly motivated and hardworking individual, who has recently completed further education, achieving excellent grades in both Maths and English. I am seeking a position within a thriving industry to build upon a keen technology orientated interest and improve upon my skills in this field. A willing learner also looking to expand upon my current skillset and expand my experience.
